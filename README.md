# 🎭 SillyTavern Avatar Drag & Resize Extension

A lightweight, third-party extension for SillyTavern that grants you full control over character and user avatars. Easily reposition them anywhere on your screen and adjust their scale on the fly.

## ✨ Features

* 🖱️ **Drag & Drop:** Click and hold any avatar to move it freely around the chat interface.
* 📜 **Scroll to Resize:** Hover over an avatar and spin your mouse wheel to instantly scale it up or down.
* 🧩 **Seamless Integration:** Works perfectly with SillyTavern's native UI and extension management.

---

## 🛠️ Installation

Follow these simple steps to install the extension into your SillyTavern setup:

1. **Place the Folder:**  
   Move or copy your extension folder into the following directory:
   ```text
   ..\SillyTavern-Launcher\SillyTavern\public\scripts\extensions\third-party\
   ```

2. **Refresh SillyTavern:**  
   Restart your SillyTavern server, or simply refresh your browser page using **`Ctrl + F5`** (or **`Cmd + Shift + R`** on macOS) to clear the cache.

3. **Verify Activation:**  
   * Open the **Extensions** menu in the SillyTavern UI.
   * Navigate to the **Manage Extensions** tab.
   * Scroll down to the bottom of the list to ensure the extension is listed and active.

Your extension is now ready to use! 🎉

---

## 🚀 Usage

* **Moving Avatars:** Left-click and hold your mouse on an avatar, then drag it to your preferred position.
* **Resizing Avatars:** Move your mouse cursor over the avatar and roll the **Mouse Scroll Wheel** up or down.
